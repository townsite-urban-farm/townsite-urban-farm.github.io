Townsite Urban Farm (TUF) is a small-scale cooperative urban farm in downtown Flagstaff, Arizona that aims to create a resilient community network through permaculture principles, collaborative learning, and social connectivity. As an accessible outdoor incubator space, TUF provides a contemporary approach and model for what is possible.
